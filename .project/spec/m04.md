# M04: Response Controls Beta

## Objective
Add narrow, high-confidence response actions that integrate with the case and evidence workflow.

## User-Visible Outcome
Selected detections can trigger controlled actions such as step-up auth, approval gating, or a share restriction workflow.

## Required Capabilities
- response action definitions and policy bindings
- case-linked approval or action history
- explicit operator and analyst visibility into why an action was triggered
- false-positive handling that can suppress or unwind supported actions

## Acceptance Criteria
- at least two response actions are usable in beta
- response actions integrate with the case lifecycle
- action history and rationale are visible to analysts

## Dependencies
- `m02`
- `m03`

## Explicitly Out Of Scope
- broad inline blocking across all sources
- autonomous actioning with no analyst or policy guardrails
- generalized SOAR platform ambitions
